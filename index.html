About Us

Advance Construction Services brings over 40 years of combined experience in civil engineering, construction inspection, and project management for both commercial and infrastructure projects across the United States and abroad.

We are recognized for our technical expertise, proactive problem-solving, and in-depth knowledge of construction codes and standards. By partnering with us, clients gain access to highly qualified professionals who add measurable value to every project phase.



  • Team

    Our team consists of seasoned professionals with extensive experience in construction management, field inspection, and quality assurance. We have successfully supported the delivery of large-scale projects including roads, bridges, utilities, transit infrastructure, and commercial developments. We provide field-driven, practical solutions that help owners and contractors stay on schedule and in compliance. .

  • Practical Solutions

    Advance Construction Services empowers clients with reliable, field-tested solutions. Through thorough assessments, expert recommendations, and hands-on guidance, we help ensure compliance, reduce risk, and protect project investments.

  • Rich Experience

    We are well-versed in WSDOT & ODOT standards, AASHTO, ASTM specifications, and local municipal codes. Our approach prioritizes clarity, coordination, and proactive quality control.

  • Collaborative Culture

    We foster a culture of collaboration, integrity, and continuous improvement. Our commitment to professional development and service excellence drives every project we support.

Services

Our core services are designed to deliver excellence and reliability at every stage of your project.

On-site Inspections

Comprehensive field inspections to ensure project compliance and quality standards are met on-site.

Constructability Review

Expert analysis of plans and specifications to identify potential construction challenges before work begins.

Change Order and Claims Review

Thorough review and evaluation of change orders and claims to protect project interests and budgets.

Construction Management

Professional oversight and coordination of all construction activities to ensure timely and successful project delivery.

Plan and Cost Review

Detailed review of project plans and budgets to ensure accuracy and cost-effectiveness.

Quality Management

Implementation of quality assurance processes to maintain high standards throughout the project lifecycle.

Document Control

Efficient management and tracking of all project documentation for easy access and compliance.

Monthly Progress Documentation

Regular reporting and documentation of project progress to keep stakeholders informed and projects on track.

Owner Representation

Acting on behalf of the owner to safeguard interests and ensure project objectives are met.

Submittal & Design Review

Careful review of submittals and designs to ensure compliance with project requirements and standards.

Closeout Documentation Inspection

Final inspection and verification of closeout documents to ensure project completion and compliance.
