About Us

At Advance Consulting Services, our leadership team brings over 40 years of combined experience in civil engineering, construction inspection, and project management across the United States and internationally. We are known for our technical expertise, proactive problem-solving, and deep knowledge of construction standards.



  • Team

    Advance Consulting Services is a team of seasoned professionals in construction management, field inspection, and quality assurance. Our background spans major infrastructure and building projects including roads, bridges, utilities, transit, and commercial developments.

  • Practical Solutions

    We provide practical, field-driven solutions that help owners and contractors deliver projects on time and in compliance with applicable standards.

  • Rich Experience

    We are well-versed in WSDOT, AASHTO, ASTM, VALSTAT, HSTM, IBC, and local municipal codes. Our team brings a detailed understanding of technical requirements while maintaining a proactive approach to coordination, problem-solving, and quality documentation.

  • Collaborative Culture

    At Advance Consulting Services, we foster a culture of collaboration, integrity, and excellence. Our team is dedicated to continuous improvement and professional development, ensuring that we deliver the highest quality services to our clients.

Services

Our core services are designed to deliver excellence and reliability at every stage of your project.

On-site Inspections

Comprehensive field inspections to ensure project compliance and quality standards are met on-site.

Constructability Review

Expert analysis of plans and specifications to identify potential construction challenges before work begins.

Change Order and Claims Review

Thorough review and evaluation of change orders and claims to protect project interests and budgets.

Construction Management

Professional oversight and coordination of all construction activities to ensure timely and successful project delivery.

Plan and Cost Review

Detailed review of project plans and budgets to ensure accuracy and cost-effectiveness.

Quality Management

Implementation of quality assurance processes to maintain high standards throughout the project lifecycle.

Document Control

Efficient management and tracking of all project documentation for easy access and compliance.

Monthly Progress Documentation

Regular reporting and documentation of project progress to keep stakeholders informed and projects on track.

Owner Representation

Acting on behalf of the owner to safeguard interests and ensure project objectives are met.

Submittal & Design Review

Careful review of submittals and designs to ensure compliance with project requirements and standards.

Closeout Documentation Inspection

Final inspection and verification of closeout documents to ensure project completion and compliance.
